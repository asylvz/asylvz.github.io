<h3 style="margin-bottom: 0.8em;">About</h3>

<p>I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Engineering at <a href="https://erbakan.edu.tr/tr/birim/bilgisayar-muhendisligi-bolumu/">Necmettin Erbakan University</a>. I am also a Research Affiliate at the <a href="https://marschall-lab.github.io/">Marschall Lab</a>, part of the Institute for Medical Biometry and Bioinformatics at Heinrich-Heine University, and a member of the <a href="http://compevo.bio.metu.edu.tr/">CompEvo Lab</a> at the Middle East Technical University (METU).</p>


<p>My primary research interests lie in the development of <strong>algorithms for bioinformatics and computational genomics</strong>, particularly in the areas of</p>
<ul style="line-height: 1.0em">
<li><em>Structural variation discovery and genotyping</em></li>
<li><em>Pangenomes</em></li>
<li><em>Ancient DNA</em></li>
</ul>

<p>I received my PhD from the Department of Computer Engineering at Bilkent University in 2018, where I worked under the supervision of Can Alkan in the <a href="http://alkanlab.org/"> Bioinformatics and Computational Genomics Group</a>. Most recently, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the <a href="https://marschall-lab.github.io/">Marschall Lab</a>, Heinrich-Heine University (2022–2024), before joining Necmettin Erbakan University.</p>
